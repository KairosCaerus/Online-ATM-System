Frequently Asked Questions and Help

How do I access my account?

By entering your username and password to login here


How do I find the nearest Bank of Rona location

Bank of Rona is an online only bank with no physical locations.


How do I close my account?

You will be able to perform this action in the account summary once all funds in your account are $0.00. You will be prompted to confirm your account deletion.


How can I withdraw money?

By visiting our online ATM and signing in with your username and ID.


How do I find my account number?

Your account number will be listed in the account summary page alongside the funds available.
